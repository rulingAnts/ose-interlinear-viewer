How to import into FLEx

  1. In FLEx, open the project you want to import into.
  2. Go to File → Import → FLExText Interlinear.
  3. Browse to the .flextext file you just downloaded and click Open.
  4. FLEx will show a preview of the import. Confirm the writing systems look correct — they should match the codes you entered above. If you see unexpected writing systems listed, cancel and double-check your codes.
  5. Click Import. The story will appear in your Texts & Words list.

After import, open the text in the interlinear editor to review and approve the word analyses. Each verse is imported as a separate paragraph.
